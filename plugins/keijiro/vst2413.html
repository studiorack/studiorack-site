VST2413 v1.0.2

By Keijiro Takahashi

FM synthesizer based on YM2413 (OPLL) emulator. YM2413 is well known as low-cost FM sound chip, used on Sega Master System and FM-PAC sound expansion cartridge for MSX. Well-suited for use in 'post 8-bit era style' chiptune music.
